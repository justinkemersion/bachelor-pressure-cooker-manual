# Recipe Planning Notes - Next 5 Recipes

## Overview
These 5 recipes expand the cookbook's flavor profiles and techniques while building on the foundational knowledge from the Initiation Recipe.

## 1. Honey Garlic Chicken Bowl (Asian-American Fusion)

### New Lego Block
**Sweet/Savory Balance + Glaze Reduction Technique**

### Why It Fits
- Familiar flavor (everyone knows honey garlic)
- Teaches sauce reduction technique
- Builds on sweet/savory balance concept (vs. acid/fat balance from balsamic recipe)

### Flavor Profile
- **Asian-American** (not authentic, but delicious)
- Sweet + savory + umami

### New Technique
- Making a thick glaze from cooking liquid
- Reduction method (reducing liquid to create sticky glaze)
- Sweet/savory balance (honey + soy sauce + garlic)

### Bachelor Appeal
- Tastes expensive
- Uses pantry staples (honey, soy sauce, garlic powder)
- No exotic ingredients needed

### Upgrades Initiation Recipe
- Teaches **sweet/savory balance** vs. acid/fat balance
- Shows how to create restaurant-quality glaze from simple ingredients

### Key Ingredients
- Honey
- Soy sauce
- Garlic powder
- Optional: Ginger powder, sesame seeds

### Potential Structure
- Phase 1: Cook chicken in honey + soy sauce + garlic liquid
- Phase 2: Add rice, cook together
- Phase 3: Reduce cooking liquid to create glaze, drizzle over finished bowl
