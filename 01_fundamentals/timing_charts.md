## Release Types

- **NR (Natural Release):** Let pressure drop naturally (10-15 minutes). Essential for rice and beans.
- **QR (Quick Release):** Manually release pressure immediately. Use for vegetables, delicate proteins.
- **10m NR:** Wait 10 minutes, then quick release remaining pressure.

## Critical Rules

1. **Never quick release rice** - Always natural release for proper texture
2. **Frozen proteins** - Add 2-4 minutes to thawed times
3. **Rice ratios** - White rice: 1:1, Brown rice: 1:1.25 (rice:water)
4. **Bean soaking** - Soak overnight OR use 1-hour quick soak method
